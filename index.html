About EduCode


EduCode is a roadmap-first learning platform built to guide you through the world of coding and technology — step by step, day by day. We don’t offer scattered lessons or random tutorials. Instead, EduCode gives you structured daily roadmaps that help you stay consistent, learn the right skills in the right order, and build real-world coding confidence. Whether you're a complete beginner or switching careers, EduCode’s roadmaps provide a clear, focused learning journey — including beginner-friendly guidance, hands-on practice, project milestones, and interview prep — all organized to keep you moving forward with purpose.
